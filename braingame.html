Brain Game

Instructions

Your mission is to give calming resources to the brain so that it comes down to a normal temperature! In the process, you will also get advice on how to restore your brain to a calming state as well. In order to drop the brain's temperature from 127 all the way to 99, give the brain water and playing music. Click on the "relaxing music" button to play music. Position the watering can over the brain by dragging it. Then, click on the "water brain" button to keep your brain hydrated! There's also a button that states "continue homework/work." Click on it as well to see what happpens!
