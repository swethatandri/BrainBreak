Brain Game

Instructions

Your mission is to give calming resources to the brain so that it comes down to a normal temperature! In order to drop the brain's temperature from 127 all the way to 97, give the brain water and playing music. Click on the "relaxing music" button to play music. Position the watering can over the brain by dragging it. Then, click on the "water brain" button to keep your brain hydrated!
